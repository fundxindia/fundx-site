FundX – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025‑01‑01

This Privacy Policy explains how FundX (a personal technology prototype owned by 《☆》) collects and uses information. FundX is used only for internal testing and education. It is not a public banking / NBFC product.

1. Data we collect

  • Basic profile: name, email address, phone number.
  • Optional KYC photos: profile photo, Aadhaar / ID photo, house photo.
  • App usage: loan requests, wallet transfers and in‑app actions.
  • Device signals (if you consent): location snapshots, gallery access for uploading photos, and SMS alerts related to banking / UPI transactions.

2. How we use this data

  • To design and test user journeys for loans, wallets and collections.
  • To verify identity and addresses inside the prototype.
  • To analyse risk behaviour for future scoring experiments.

3. Data sharing

FundX does not sell personal data to third‑party advertisers. Access to data is restricted to the small internal team operating this prototype.

4. Storage & security

All data is stored in a private Supabase (PostgreSQL) project. We use standard industry practices to protect this data, but no system can be 100% secure.

5. Your choices

  • You can withdraw location / gallery / SMS permissions from your phone settings.
  • You can request deletion of your test account by emailing us.

6. Contact

For any privacy questions, please write to fundxindia@gmail.com.
