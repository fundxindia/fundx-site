FundX – Refund Policy

Last updated: 2025‑01‑01

FundX is a closed prototype and does not handle real customer money. All “wallet balances” and “transactions” are simulated entries for testing. Therefore, no real payments or refunds are processed through this app.

1. No real payments

If you see any transaction inside FundX, it only represents a test record in our database. It is not linked to a bank account, UPI ID or card network.

2. Test refunds

In case of any issue with a simulated transaction, the internal admin may adjust the wallet balance inside the prototype. This has no impact on your real bank balance.

3. Contact

For questions about this refund policy, please email fundxindia@gmail.com.
